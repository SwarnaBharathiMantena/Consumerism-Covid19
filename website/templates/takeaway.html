Key Takeaways


  • About 50 percent of the respondents were not aware of the terms consumerism and post-consumerism.
  • The respondents’ expenditure seem to have varied across the industries due to COVID 19 in the following way
    Increase No change Decrease
    Banking, Insurance, and Online Service platform Food and Staples Retailing, Education, and Real Estate Capital Goods, Consumer Apparel, and Media & Entertainment
  • People seem to not like "Remote Everything" kind of lifestyle.
  • Online Service Platforms, Media and Entertainment, and Real Estate Industries show positive trends from May 17th 2020 to June 17th 2020.
  • Health Care Industry shows positive trend from June 1st 2020.
  • Majority of the respondents are inclined towards post-consumerism and are not ready to go back to their usual self post covid19. However, it is interesting to see that people don’t think there would be much growth in post-consumerist lifestyle in the society as a whole.
